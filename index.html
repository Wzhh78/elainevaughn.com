About Elaine Vaughn

Elaine Vaughn, a novelist, painter, and Lyricist based in Bremen, Germany.

To me, art is not confined to a single medium; it is a fluid, interconnected canvas where stories, colors, and sounds breathe together. My creative journey thrives on this beautiful overlap, what I call intertextuality.

My novel, Viola Bennet, serves as the emotional soil for my entire artistic universe. The verses born within its pages have been woven into copyright-registered musical compositions, giving a melodic voice to the text on global platforms like Spotify and YouTube. Meanwhile, my oil paintings translate these unseen inner landscapes into tactile textures, light, and shadow.

Whether through words, melodies, or a stroke of oil on canvas, my work is a continuous search for the deep, unspoken dimensions of human emotion and freedom. Welcome to this space where these creative rivers meet.
